Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a bike service usually take?

Service times vary depending on the level of service and our current workload. Basic tune-ups typically take 24-48 hours, while more comprehensive services may take 3-5 business days. We'll provide an estimated completion time when you drop off your bike.

Do you offer bike fitting services?

Yes, we offer professional bike fitting services to ensure your bike is adjusted to your body proportions for optimal comfort and performance. Our basic fitting is included with new bike purchases, and comprehensive fitting services are available by appointment.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unused, unaltered merchandise within 30 days of purchase with original receipt. Bikes can be returned within 14 days if in original condition. Special orders and sale items may have different return policies. See our complete return policy for details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs fees, taxes, and duties that may apply upon delivery.

Can I test ride bikes before purchasing?

Absolutely! We encourage test rides to ensure you find the perfect bike. Visit our store with a valid ID to test ride any bike in our inventory. Our staff will help you select appropriate bikes based on your needs and preferences.
